About Us

A nurturing school with Assamese roots and future-ready habits

Hillview School is an editable placeholder identity for a private school in Assam. The school is presented as a sincere, locally trusted institution that serves students from Assam and the wider Northeast with academic discipline, values, and holistic growth.

Our approach balances classroom learning, practical activities, co-curricular participation, digital familiarity, and respect for Assamese culture. The aim is realistic and important: help students prepare for future opportunities while staying connected to their community.

Academics

Clear teaching, regular assessment, reading habits, and steady improvement.

Discipline

Punctuality, respectful conduct, responsibility, and care for school routines.

Culture

Minimal gamosa-inspired patterns, Bihu moments, tea garden motifs, and community pride.

Our School

Mission & Vision

Hillview School is established to prettify knowledge among the students to enhance their educational standard to the national level. It aims at becoming one of the best on the educational map of Assam.Emphasis will therefore be both on its academic as well as in non-academic performance and effort at sensitizing the students in to becoming disciplined citizens infused with a sense of purpose,diligence and competitive orientation attuned to the challenging environment prevailing in the country

Our School

Objectives

The School Environment plays an important role in making students learn positively. Hillview School compound is blessed with green surroundings enabling students to be close to Mother Nature. Principal's Message

Character, leadership, and lifelong learning

Welcome to our school community. We believe every learner deserves patient guidance, strong values, and opportunities to discover their abilities. Our teachers work to build confidence, curiosity, discipline, and leadership through everyday school life.

As Assam grows with new opportunities, our students must be rooted in kindness, integrity, and cultural respect while becoming comfortable with technology and modern learning.
